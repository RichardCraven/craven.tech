MOBIUS DIMENSION is a real time multiplayer space combat game, currently in Alpha release. It is currently free to play via the browser.
Players control a ship in a semi-sandbox universe that must fight to survive in a dangerous envionment. Each player-controlled ship also has a small fleet of squadron ships that obey players' commands.
I developed the AI systems, weapons and key commands, UI components, and game logic. I also made all the sound effects and music. I work with my friend who built the back end foundation and an artist who makes the in-game models.
CAHOOTS is a social collaboration platform developed with Angular 1.5 and NodeJS on the backend.
I have a passion for creative projects. In the past I've worked on various projects and found myself wishing I could assemble a team of people to share my vision. Unable to find a local hub for creative people I took it upon myself to build one.
The app has gone through several revisions to improve the aesthetic and UX. The release target is March 2018. Stay tuned for updates!
